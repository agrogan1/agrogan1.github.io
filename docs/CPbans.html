Corporal Punishment Bans

Country Level Bans on Corporal Punishment

Background

70 countries have instituted country wide bans upon the use of corporal punishment with children. The most recent country to institute a ban is Switzerland.

Information on countries that have banned corporal punishment available from endcorporalpunishment.org
