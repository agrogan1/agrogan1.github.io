Background

A second attempt to make a beautiful graph with Stata.

I make use of the /// notation which allows me to spread the graph along multiple lines.

Get The Data

. use "https://github.com/agrogan1/newstuff/raw/master/categorical/glm/penguins.dta", 
> clear

Make The Graph

. twoway /// twoway graph
> (scatter flipper_length body_mass_g, mcolor(%50)) /// scatter plot w/ transparency
> (lowess flipper_length body_mass_g), /// lowess smoohter
> by(species, title("Penguin Flipper Length by Body Mass")) /// by species and title
> scheme(michigan) // Michigan graph scheme

. graph export beautiful-penguins.png, width(2000) replace
