Visualizing Multilevel Models

An In Development Tutorial.

Andy Grogan-Kaylor
2023-04-24

I have developed a book draft on multilevel modeling, entitled Multilevel Thinking. The book draft discusses multilevel modeling in the context of cross cultural, or international, research.

I have been thinking more about one of the ideas in that book draft: multilevel models as the exploration of variation and diversity.

With those ideas in mind, I am developing a tutorial on visualizing multilevel models.
